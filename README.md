# ControlForge Community

ControlForge Community is an open-source governance intelligence and audit automation platform designed to help IT auditors, GRC practitioners, and security governance teams automate repetitive governance workflows, visualize governance posture, and generate executive-ready governance reporting.

Originally built to reduce manual audit effort during user access reviews and ITGC testing, the platform has evolved into a governance operations and intelligence engine capable of:

* Automating governance analytics
* Generating executive governance reports
* Evaluating governance posture and maturity
* Tracking governance trends over time
* Prioritizing remediation activities
* Producing governance KPI dashboards
* Visualizing governance risk concentration
* Orchestrating phased remediation roadmaps

---

# Vision

ControlForge Community is the public/open-source edition of the ControlForge platform.

The Community Edition focuses on:

* Audit automation
* Governance analytics
* Executive reporting
* Governance visualization
* Educational and portfolio-grade governance tooling
* Synthetic enterprise audit datasets

Advanced enterprise intelligence capabilities, integrations, orchestration systems, and AI-assisted governance reasoning are reserved for the future enterprise platform.
